B2B Operations – Scale Bitcoin-Native Payments

About Us

Eulen builds crypto-financial infrastructure for the sovereign individual — payments, tokenized settlement, and cross-border liquidity rails for the Global South.

Our core product is DePix: a privacy-centric payment token issued through banking nodes, operating on Bitcoin infrastructure (Liquid Network). We distribute through a growing B2B2C partner network and we're profitable.

We don't care about titles or fancy resumes. We care about results.

The Role

We're looking for a B2B Operations professional to support our partner network as it scales. You'll be a key interface between Eulen and our partners — onboarding them, keeping them running, and making sure the operation stays clean.

This is a high-ownership role in a small, high-performance team.

What You'll Own

  • Curation, onboarding, and prospecting of new partners
  • Compliance routines and support for anti-fraud processes
  • Strategic and continuous communication with active partners
  • Maintenance and optimization of internal workflows and documentation
  • Operational insights fed back to Product and Engineering

Who We're Looking For

  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Comfortable working independently and asynchronously
  • Familiarity with financial operations, fintech, and/or crypto (strong plus)
  • Experience with compliance or anti-fraud processes (valued)
  • Fluent in Portuguese; English proficiency highly preferred
  • You build processes, not just follow them
