Senior DevOps Engineer – Cloud, Reliability & Incident Response

Location: 100% Remote

Company: Eulen.app

About Us

Eulen builds crypto-financial infrastructure for the sovereign individual — payments, tokenized settlement, and cross-border liquidity rails for the Global South.

Our core product is DePix: a privacy-centric payment token issued through banking nodes, operating on Bitcoin infrastructure (Liquid Network). We run systems that must stay up under pressure. We're profitable and growing.

We don't care about degrees or fancy resumes. We care about production results.

The Role

This is a hands-on Senior DevOps role for someone who owns production reliability — not just monitors it. You'll be the person who improves our operational posture, hardens our infrastructure, and makes it safe to deploy fast.

Your mission: increase availability and resiliency, reduce operational risk, automate everything possible.

What You'll Own

  • Cloud infrastructure (Azure/AWS): design, implement, and operate production environments
  • Infrastructure as Code: standardize and automate provisioning and deployments (Terraform + CI/CD)
  • Observability / SRE: metrics, alerting, dashboards, SLI/SLO definition
  • Incident response: lead troubleshooting under pressure, run postmortems, implement preventive improvements
  • Bitcoin/crypto basics: enough to operate safely in an environment that touches crypto components (specialist knowledge not required)

Who We're Looking For

  • 10+ years active experience in DevOps / SRE / Platform / Infrastructure
  • 5+ years hands-on with Azure or AWS (or equivalent multi-cloud depth)
  • Real track record handling production incidents — calm, fast, pragmatic
  • Strong IaC skills (Terraform or equivalent)
  • High ownership, clear communication, bias toward production results
  • You ship robust infrastructure. You don't debate it.
